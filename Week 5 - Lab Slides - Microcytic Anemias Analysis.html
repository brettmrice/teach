Within each tab is how your parameter evaluations compare to mine. Please note that your evaluation is compared to a range of values around my evaluation. My accepted ranges are narrower when my counts are low and wider when my counts are high

For example, if my WBC Est evaluation is 6.0, then I assess your evaluation as “Good” if you report a WBC estimate between 3 and 9. Values below my accepted range have a yellow label and values above a red label

For WBC Estimates, WBC Differentials, and PLT Estimates, I provide a “# SD”. This represents the number of standard deviations away from the accepted range mean. I define my accepted range as the mean plus/minus two standard deviations. Any reported value below/above 2 SD was marked as “Too Low”/“Too High”

Each parameter set is presented two ways. First, a summary of your “Individual” analysis of each parameter. Second, a summary of the “Class” frequencies for each parameter

“Inf SD” means I called zero and you called more than zero

Notice the analyses by cell types are separated by tabs (right under “Analyses” header)
